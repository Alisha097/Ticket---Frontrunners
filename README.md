CHALLENGE:

A core aspect of Wise's mission is transparency — unlike legacy banks, Wise transparently discloses the fees that it charges for international payments. Build a project that takes something which is opaque or obscure and make it more transparent. Some ideas might be:

Displaying tickets to gigs and events including the pesky yet unavoidable booking fees

Making it easier to compare flights including all the extra fees that budget airlines add on top

Making government data released under the Freedom of Information Act more accessible

PROBLEM:

Many Ticket platforms (for concerts and live events) tend to have opaque and obscure view of additional fees that we online get to know about until we reach the checkout.

SOLUTION:

Give a transparent view of the fees with the base price and the overall actual price.
